    Idea Lab

    Idea lab is an idea testing app for innovators to quickly organize their idea research, create research portfolios, test their ideas, and review feedback on their ideas.

    Role

    UX research & design - designing an art gallery tour booking app from conception to delivery

    Duration

    December 2021 - April 2022

    Team

    Uche Orjinta

    The problem

    Aspiring entrepreneurs & innovators with ideas do not have a way to organize their idea’s research, and test their ideas’ viability in real-time to enable them understand their idea better before commiting financially to their ideas development.

    The goal

    Design an app that will let users easily organize their idea research, create research portfolios, test their ideas, and review feedback on their ideas.

    Starting point

    Understanding the user

    Jane is a recent graduate from university, who wants to know how to start a business and test her ideas, so that she can decided if her idea is ready to be launched as a profitable business.

    1) Persona info

    Market Survey

    Competitive Audit

    I audited 3 competitors; 'direct & indirect' to compare their product features & services and identify the value propositions offered by each competitor.

    2) Competitive analysis of primary & secondar competitors

    Ideation

    Paper wireframes

    I did a quick ideation exercise to come up with ideas for how to address gaps that were identified in the competitive audit. our focus was on a simple way to organize & test ideas.

    3) Crazy 8 to paper wireframes

    Starting the design

    Digital wireframes

    Using the completed set of digital wireframes, I created a low-fidelity prototype. The primary user flow I connected was orgaizing ideas using pre-filled templates for business ideas, e-book manuscripts, and project management guides.

    4) lofi-prototyping

    Usability study

    Usability study findings

    4 out of 5 users that tested the prototype wanted to be able to set their own idea testing metrics/goals to enable them easily understand the results/feedback after testing their ideas.

    5) Mobile version-Usability study findings

    Design Challenges

    Mobile app design challenges

    Design changes included allowing users easily upload a variery of content type directly onto prefilled templates, and to achieve this i created a template screen to showcase all templates available, including a learn more button to provide users with more information about how to use each template type available.

    6) Mobile design challenge

    Going forward

    responsive designs

    Users of Idea Lab would use both their mobile devices and deskop computers to access the app, and this made me create responsive designs for both mobile and desktop to accomodation both user needs.

    7) Responsive designs for mobile & web

    Prototyping

    High fidelity prototype

    The high-fidelity prototype followed the same user flow as the low-fidelity prototype, including changes made after the usability study.

    8) Mobile version

    Conclusion

    Take aways

    Overall we succeded in designing an app that helps them organize their ideas better and easily test their new ideas remotely.
